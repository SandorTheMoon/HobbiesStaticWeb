    • KABANERI OF THE IRON FORTRESS

    8.5 / 10

    Amidst the industrial revolution, a virus birthed fearsome creatures called "Kabane" that hunted humans. With nearly impenetrable steel hearts, they were hard to kill, and their bites could turn victims into Kabane. Fortified enclaves like Hinomoto Island were protected by colossal walls. Young men like Ikoma built heavily-armored trains to transport people safely within the city.
