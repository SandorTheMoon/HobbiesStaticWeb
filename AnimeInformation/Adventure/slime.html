THAT TIME I GOT REINCARNATED AS A SLIME

9 / 10

Tokyo corporate worker Satoru Mikami is reborn as a shape-shifting slime after a fatal attack. He befriends Veldora, a powerful sealed monster, and vows to break the seal, receiving divine protection and a new name, Rimuru Tempest. Rimuru embarks on a journey to explore the world, where his actions reshape both the world and his own destiny.

Studio : 8-bit

Type : TV Series

Release date : October 2, 2018
