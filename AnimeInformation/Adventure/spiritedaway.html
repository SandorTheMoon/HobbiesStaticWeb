    • SPIRITED AWAY

    9 / 10

    At the sight of an abandoned amusement park on the way to their new residence, 10-year-old Chihiro Ogino was far from thrilled. Being stubborn, spoiled, and inexperienced, she resented change. However, things took a turn when she cautiously ventured into the park, only to discover bizarre happenings after dusk. Her parents got turned into pigs, and she encountered ghostly apparitions. Unbeknownst to her, Chihiro had stumbled into the spirit realm and was now trapped.
