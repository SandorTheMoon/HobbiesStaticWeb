    • WATAMOTE: NO MATTER HOW I LOOK AT IT, IT'S YOU GUYS' FAULT I'M UNPOPULAR!

    9 / 10

    After experiencing 50 virtual high school lives and dating over 100 virtual boys, Tomoko Kuroki believes she's ready for her first year of real high school. However, she soon discovers that reality is quite different. In truth, she's an introverted and socially awkward girl, but she's oblivious to it. With the assistance of her best friend, Yuu Naruse, and the support of her brother, Tomoki, Tomoko tries to navigate the challenges of high school life.

    Type : TV Series
