Kousei Arima had earned the title of the "Human Metronome" with his unparalleled piano skills, courtesy of his strict upbringing and intensive training. His numerous triumphs had made him a darling of both music lovers and his colleagues. However, his mother's sudden death left Kousei reeling with trauma, rendering him unable to hear the sweet melodies of a piano, and causing him to withdraw from performing.
